Harold Shuckstotter

Founder and CEO of Shuckstotter Technologies

Born in 1954, Harold Shuckstotter started off his career as a lowly information technology assistant. After seeing the poor security practices of the organization he was working for, he created a new company - Shuckstotter Technologies - dedicated to making security a priority of not just the company's products, but the company itself. Shuckstotter Technologies has been very successful with Harold as our CEO, and hopefully with the debut of our flagship product H.A.R.O.L.D. (High Acuity Recursive Open-source Logging Daemon), our success will only continue to mount.
